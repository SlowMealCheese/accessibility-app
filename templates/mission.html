Our mission

People with disabilities often have difficulty finding places that are accessible - despite many efforts, an inability to determine whether stores and restaurants are accessible for them increases the quality of life for those with disabilities. This hack is a proof-of-concept of a larger system in which those with disabilities and allies of the disabled can post and receive information about accomodations at specific businesses.
